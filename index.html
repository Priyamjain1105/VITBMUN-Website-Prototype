What is Model United Nations?

Model United Nations (MUN) conferences simulate the workings of the United Nations, with students representing various countries, organizations, or individuals. The aim is to solve global problems through research, public speaking, debating, and writing skills. MUN teaches critical thinking, teamwork, and leadership, while also honing negotiation, conflict resolution, and cooperation abilities.
